Target Audience

The targeted audience for the Pleasant Grove Chamber of Commerce is anyone interested to learn about the town's amenities. The chamber also focuses on empowering business and specially small business owners and has special programs and training created specially for them. The site has information on all the events happening in the city throughout the year.


Personas

Sofia Smith

Age: 27

Sofia is a real state agent that recently moved to Pleasant Grove. She is looking to become familiar with this new county and would love to connect with others as she learns of the different attractions this beautiful town has to offer. Sofia loves outdoors activities and is eager to get to know every hiking track available. She is specially dedicated to her profession and would love to connect with other self employed women and business owners. She spends decent time on her computer but loves to be able to access her most important websites on her mobile as she is normally on the go.

Jacob Rondan

Age: 32

Jacob recently became a business owner. His company specializes in repairs and installation of Heating and Air Conditioning units. Jacob wants to reach out to more customers and is looking for ways to improve his business. He is very talented at what he does but could use some help in the administrative aspect of his business. He usually carries with him a tablet and this is where he mostly accesses the information he needs as he is on the go.
